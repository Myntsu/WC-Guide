About this build

My personal vision of Wind Cutter is Dungeon farming, such as Einbech 3, Juperos, Thanatos, Odin 4, etc.

Pros:

  • Somewhat versatile
  • Cheap early & midgame
  • 100% DEF PEN

Cons:

  • Agi-up mobs
  • Short range
  • Endgame expensive
  • Not high DMG

Stats

Status

My recommendation is to leave Str & Dex at 120 at all times, and the rest at 100 minimum (buffs considered).

Have extra points?

Agi for extra damage due to Black Ribbon.

Vit to survive longer & potion effectiveness.

Int if you lack or run out of SP.

Luk to increase your hit rate and damage a little bit.
