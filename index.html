Hello! My nickname is Myndi.

I have been playing Ragnarok for around 2 years, and Wind Cutter almost ever since.

My main goal is to have fun and help as many people as I can, so without further a do.

Hopefully this build is as helpful for you as it was for me.

About this build

My personal vision of Wind Cutter is Dungeon farming, such as Einbech 3, Juperos, Thanatos, Odin 4, etc.

Pros:

  • Somewhat versatile
  • Cheap early & midgame
  • 100% DEF PEN

Cons:

  • Agi-up mobs
  • Short range
  • Endgame expensive
  • Not high DMG
